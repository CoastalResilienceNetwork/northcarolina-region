Data Notes

Current Habitat: Source - NC Emergency Management, North Carolina Sea Level Rise Impact Study, June 2014. Location and acreage of baseline habitat types were derived from United States Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS) National Wetland Inventory (NWI) data.
Future Habitat/Marsh Migration Data: Source - NC Emergency Management, North Carolina Sea Level Rise Impact Study, June 2014. Marsh evolution was evaluated using the Sea Level Affecting Marsh Model (SLAMM) Version 6.1. The SLAMM analysis was performed for sea level rise scenarios of 40 cm, 60 cm, 80 cm, and 100 cm.
